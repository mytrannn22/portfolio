About Me

Over two years working in Hospitality and one year working in Industrial Real Estate, I often worked with data in the form of numbers, reports, and KPIs, but I felt like there was a missing piece that I needed for my personal growth. I knew I could take more from data, so at the age of 26, I decided to take a break and focus on learning new skills: data analysis and visualization.

The more I learned and practiced, the more certain I felt that this was exactly what I was looking for. I love playing with data, making sense of it, telling stories about it, and using it to make better evidence-based decisions.

Now I am looking for a role in data analytics and business intelligence, where I can learn, grow, and contribute value by helping businesses make better decisions and achieve their goals.
